# This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of a Chocolate Store Analysis, focusing on sales performance, store distribution, and geographic trends. The key performance indicators (KPIs) and insights:
- Total Revenue: 2.43K
- Total Orders: 100
- Total Quantity Sold: 296 units
- Total Store Count: 100
The following analysis examines the chocolate store's performance
Store Type: Airport locations made the highest revenue, significantly outperforming Mall, Online, and Retail channels, which are relatively equal in their contributions.

Trends Over Time: The "Total Revenue by order_date" line chart shows a steady decline in revenue over the period shown, starting near 300 and dropping sharply toward the end.  The store is concentrated in North America and Europe, as shown on the map visualization. Canada and the UK have the highest number of stores (approaching 20 each) while Germany has the fewest stores among the highlighted regions.
 While Airport stores generate the most revenue, the store count is highest in Canada and the UK.
